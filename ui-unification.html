mParticle's Journey to UI Consistency cross Platforms

The Project
In 2023 mParticle shifted its focus towards helping the Marketing to accelerate their customer data strategy. To enhance its current offerings, mParticle acquired Indicative, a customer journey analytics platform, and Vidora, an AI personalization platform for customer data.

The integration of these platforms posed a challenge: the three platforms offered vastly different user experience both from customer workflow and look & feel perspective. This disjointed experience negatively impacted customer acquisition process for the sales team.

I was responsible for the roadmap design, focused on integrating workflows across three apps and unifying experience overall.
[Insert Image]
The Results
In just under four months, we established a whole new Figma design system (with the help of an agency), translated it into a React front-end library, unified navigation, and streamlined core flows, including login and onboarding. We also updated the styling across all three properties, eliminating core blockers from sales demos.
