Cashflow Intelligence

Project Overview

The Cash Flow Intelligence application provides non-technical stakeholders with cash flows insights and machine learning based cash flow forecasts. Minimum Marketable Product featured an overview page that allowed users to explore historical data and generated forecasts based on series of settings.

My Role

As a part of Rose's Digital team I was responsible for working with J.P. Morgan Product and Design teams to define MMP (Minimum Marketable Product), create implementation roadmap and design deliverables.

Execution and Outcomes

During this project, I worked closely with Rose's Digital Front-End Engineering Team and J.P.Morgan Backend and Data Science teams to evaluate the feasibility of early UX concepts provided by the design team. The proposal was continuously refined based on internal customer feedback, with scope adjustments to ensure the project timeline would be met. Once MMP was approved, I worked through the detailed designs, addressing edge cases and applied J.P.Morgan's design patterns and style guides.
